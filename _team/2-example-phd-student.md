> **This is a template/example team member page.** Duplicate this file in the `_team/` folder, rename it, and edit the front matter and body to add a real group member. Delete this file once you have real members. Each file in `_team/` automatically becomes its own sub-page (e.g. `/team/2-example-phd-student/`).

Short bio of the student goes here: research focus, background, and interests. You can include links such as:

- **Email:** [xxx](mailto:name@huskers.unl.edu)
- **Research:** brief description of their project within the group.

**Front matter fields you can set:**

- `title` — the person's name (shown as the heading and the link on the Team page)
- `role_group` — one of: `Principal Investigator`, `Ph.D. Students`, `M.S. Students`, `Undergraduate Researchers`, `Alumni` (controls which section they appear under)
- `position` — their title/role line
- `order` — sort order within their group (lower numbers first)
- `excerpt` — one-line summary shown on the Team listing page
- `header.teaser` — photo filename in `/images/` (or a full URL); shown as a circular thumbnail
